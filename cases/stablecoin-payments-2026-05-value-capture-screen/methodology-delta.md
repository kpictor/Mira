## Why The Change Is Needed

Stablecoin themes can look obvious at the adoption level. The hard question is whether a public company captures durable equity economics after:

- reserve asset quality
- short-rate sensitivity
- distribution or partner revenue share
- redemption and liquidity risk
- issuer regulatory status
- AML/BSA and sanctions obligations
- payment usage, take rate and margin bridge
- valuation expectations

These variables are specific enough that they should not be buried inside generic product, industry or regulatory analysis.

## Ordinary Vs Workflow Result

Ordinary research risk:

- conclude that regulatory clarity and stablecoin growth make all direct and indirect stablecoin beneficiaries attractive.

Workflow result:

- broad theme is `industry_map_first_with_single_equity_candidates`
- direct issuers and distribution partners are first single-equity candidates
- card networks and wallets are watch-only pending usage and take-rate bridge
- banks and blockchain infrastructure require clearer value-capture evidence

## Methodology Judgment

Keep the change.

This overlay is likely useful for:

- stablecoin issuer cases
- exchange/distribution partner cases
- payment network and wallet cases
- tokenized treasury / tokenized cash themes
- bank/custodian cases where stablecoins create deposit or custody risk

It should remain a triggered overlay, not a default requirement for every long-term thesis.
