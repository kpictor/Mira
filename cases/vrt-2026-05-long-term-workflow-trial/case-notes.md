## Intermediate Notes

### Business And Industry

VRT is a high-purity public expression of AI data-center infrastructure. Q1 2026 reported net sales grew 30%, organic sales grew 23%, and Americas organic sales grew 44.3%, supported by strong data-center demand.

Compared with ETN, VRT has cleaner thematic exposure but less diversified ballast. This makes the thesis easier to understand but potentially more vulnerable to theme deceleration or valuation compression.

### Financial Quality

Q1 2026 adjusted operating margin was 20.8%, up 430 bps. Operating cash flow was $767M and adjusted free cash flow was $653M. FY2026 adjusted diluted EPS guidance midpoint is $6.35.

The margin and cash-flow evidence is strong. The open issue is durability: how much is structural operating leverage versus cycle timing and working-capital benefit?
