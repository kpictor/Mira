## Why The Change Is Needed

AI power and nuclear themes can look obvious at the demand level. The hard question is whether a public company captures value after:

- PPA economics
- offtaker credit
- regulatory approvals
- interconnection treatment
- grid cost allocation
- commercial operation timing
- restart or construction execution
- valuation expectations

These variables are specific enough that they should not be buried inside generic macro or industry analysis.

## Ordinary Vs Workflow Result

## Methodology Judgment

Keep the change.

This overlay is likely useful for:

- nuclear and SMR themes
- data-center power contracts
- IPP and utility cases
- grid-constrained electrification themes
- regulated infrastructure projects where customer demand is visible but approval and cost recovery are uncertain

It should remain a triggered overlay, not a default requirement for every long-term thesis.
