# Methodology Delta

- methodology_under_test: `roadmap-to-bottleneck-recursion`
- case: `CPO / silicon photonics`
- validation_mode: `live methodology trial`
- baseline: ordinary `industry-concept-analysis` + `supply-chain`
- result: `positive_partial`
- research_cutoff_date: 2026-06-01

## What Changed Versus Ordinary Industry Mapping

Ordinary industry mapping would likely produce a broad chain:

`NVIDIA / hyperscalers -> switch ASICs -> CPO / silicon photonics -> optical components -> fiber/connectors -> candidate optical stocks`

The recursion method produced a narrower ladder:

`NVIDIA AI factory roadmap -> Spectrum-X / Quantum-X Photonics -> CPO package -> photonic engine -> external light source -> InP laser manufacturing -> InP substrate/epi and laser equipment`

It also produced a second ladder:

`NVIDIA AI factory roadmap -> optical fabric scale -> fiber attach -> fiber/connectivity manufacturing footprint`

## Incremental Value

- It downgraded generic optical exposure and raised external lasers / InP manufacturing as the first validation path.
- It separated strategic bottlenecks with poor public purity, such as TSMC COUPE, from more testable public proxies, such as COHR, LITE, VECO, AXTI and GLW.
- It converted "CPO is a theme" into concrete refresh triggers: laser orders, InP equipment backlog, COUPE production timing, fiber/connectivity capacity and company margin conversion.
- It made the stock handoff stricter by requiring exposure purity and financial transmission before any single-equity thesis.

## What Failed Or Remains Weak

- Search coverage is still first-pass and official-source heavy.
- The package does not yet include sell-side estimates, valuation expectations, historical analogues or customer deployment data.
- It does not prove that any company is mispriced.
- Private firms may capture a large share of silicon-photonics value, weakening public equity mapping.
- The current evidence supports roadmap and bottleneck plausibility more than revenue and margin conversion.

## Trial Decision

Keep `roadmap-to-bottleneck-recursion` under `trial`.

This case supports the method as a useful enhancer, but not as adopted methodology. Adoption would require:

1. One company-level handoff case proving different actionability versus normal supply-chain work.
2. One follow-through refresh after a material CPO / photonics earnings or product update.
3. One historical failure backtest showing how the method avoids false-positive bottleneck stocks.

- Pluggable optics, LPO or another architecture weakens the need for CPO at scale.
