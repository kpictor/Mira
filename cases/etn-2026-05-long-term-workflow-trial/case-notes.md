## Intermediate Notes

### Business And Industry

ETN is a credible supplier/enabler in AI power infrastructure. The strongest facts are in Electrical Americas: $3.6B Q1 sales, 20% YoY sales growth, 14% organic growth, 42% organic growth in rolling twelve-month orders and 44% backlog growth versus March 2025.

The data-center thesis has also been reinforced through portfolio action. Fibrebond supports modular power enclosures for data-center and related customers; Boyd Thermal adds thermal components and systems for data center, aerospace and other markets.

### Financial Quality

Q1 2026 total sales were $7.451B and organic growth was 10%. Adjusted EPS was $2.81, and FY2026 adjusted EPS guidance is $13.05-$13.50. GAAP EPS is lower because acquisition/divestiture, restructuring and intangible amortization charges are material.

Debt and acquisition accounting matter. The Boyd Thermal and Ultra PCS deals increased asset base and debt, and they make ROIC/future FCF conversion more important than revenue growth alone.
