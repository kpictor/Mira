## Why COHR First

COHR is the cleanest first handoff from the CPO bottleneck ladder because NVIDIA and Coherent announced a multiyear strategic agreement, NVIDIA committed a $2 billion investment, and the agreement explicitly covers advanced laser and optical networking products, capacity and access rights.

This does not make COHR an automatic buy. The handoff asks whether a real CPO / external-laser bottleneck can transmit into company-level revenue, margin, cash flow and valuation support after a large market rerating.

## Current Decision

`watch_only_pending_financial_transmission`

COHR has strong roadmap validation and real operating momentum, but the current handoff stops short of a full thesis upgrade because:

- Datacenter & Communications growth is strong.
- NVIDIA strategic validation is unusually direct.
- InP / 6-inch platform and U.S. manufacturing scale fit the bottleneck ladder.
- Cash conversion is weak.
- Inventory, receivables and CapEx are absorbing the growth.
- Market cap has already expanded enough that expectation burden matters.

## Refresh Conditions

- COHR FY2026 Q4 results or guide.
- New NVIDIA / hyperscaler optical product or capacity disclosures.
- Evidence that CPO / NPO revenue timing moves materially earlier or later.
- Gross margin exits or misses the 39%-41% Q4 guide range.
- Inventory and accounts receivable continue rising faster than revenue.
