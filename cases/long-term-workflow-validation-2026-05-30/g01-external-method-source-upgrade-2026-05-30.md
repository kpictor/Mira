# G01 External Method Source Upgrade

- upgrade_date: 2026-05-30
- gate: `G01 external_method_scan`
- status: partial_pass_improved
- source_audit: `g01-external-method-source-audit.csv`

## Purpose

The initial methodology scan had useful public institutional sources but weak
Chinese-language and practitioner coverage. This upgrade adds public Asian /
Chinese practitioner sources and one long-horizon institutional macro process
source to reduce that gap before external review.

## Facts

- Hillhouse's public long-term materials support patience, strategic ambiguity
  tolerance, exceptional entrepreneur assessment and long-horizon ownership.
- Hillhouse's current public approach page describes research as finding
  obscure signals, connecting emerging trends and anticipating future needs.
- Ping An Asset Management's Chinese public page describes equity investing
  as fundamental-analysis-centered, combining bottom-up stock selection,
  top-down industry research, qualitative and quantitative research, financial
  analysis and grassroots research.
- J.P. Morgan Asset Management's Long-Term Capital Market Assumptions provide a
  10-15 year institutional long-horizon prior for macro/regime assumptions.
- J.P. Morgan's long-term investing principles add behavioral and horizon
  discipline but are not a single-equity methodology.

## Inferences

- The six-lens workflow is still directionally consistent with public
  institutional and practitioner priors: demand, product, macro, industry,
  company execution and valuation expectations are all defensible as a
  multi-variable long-term thesis structure.
- The Chinese source coverage is now materially better than the first scan,
  especially for the combination of fundamental research, top-down industry
  work and grassroots evidence.
- Public buyside sources still rarely disclose enough detail to prove exact
  analyst workflow quality. External reviewer challenge remains necessary.

## Judgment

G01 should move from `partial_pass` to `partial_pass_improved`, not to
`pass_external`.

The upgrade improves source breadth, but it does not remove the need for G06.
An independent reviewer should still decide whether the public-source basis is
adequate for sharing the workflow with institutional colleagues.

## Remaining Limits

- Private buyside process detail remains undercovered because non-public
  investment manuals and analyst training materials are not available.
- Chinese practitioner materials are public and broad, not case-level
  underwriting memos.
- This upgrade does not validate Mira case outcomes; it only improves external
  method-source coverage.
