# Ordinary Research vs Workflow Comparison

- comparison_date: 2026-05-30
- methodology: `long-term-integrated-thesis`
- status: internal_evidence_of_incremental_value

## Purpose

The public-grade bar requires a clear comparison against ordinary Mira research-loop output.

This comparison asks:

- Did the workflow change the conclusion?
- Did it downgrade actionability?
- Did it expose a source gap or stop rule that ordinary narrative research would likely miss?
- Did it create a reusable patch?

## Summary

Across the current validation set, the workflow does add incremental value.

It does not merely make memos longer. It repeatedly changes the research state from thematic attraction to constrained actionability:

- ETN and VRT: from AI-infrastructure beneficiary to watch-only pending expectation map.
- CRM: from real AI product traction to watch-only pending product-to-company monetization bridge.
- LLY: from best-in-class GLP-1 winner to watch-only pending expectation map and realized-price refresh.
- TDOC and PTON: from pandemic-era growth extrapolation to earlier downgrade triggers.
- Humanoid robotics: from hot-theme stock picking to `industry_map_first`.
- Nuclear/SMR/AI power: from broad nuclear beneficiary basket to `industry_map_first` plus contract/regulatory-gated single-equity candidates.
- Stablecoins/tokenized payments: from broad regulatory/adoption beneficiary basket to `industry_map_first` plus reserve/regulatory-gated single-equity candidates.
- Defense autonomy/drones: from broad defense-autonomy beneficiary basket to `industry_map_first` plus procurement/program-gated single-equity candidates.

## What This Proves

The workflow has passed an internal incremental-value test.

Evidence:

- Every completed live case changed actionability, not just prose structure.
- Historical failure cases show earlier downgrade triggers than postmortem impairment/collapse evidence.
- The humanoid robotics screen proves the method can say "do not pick a stock yet."

## What This Does Not Prove

This is not final public-grade evidence.

Remaining limits:

- The comparison is internal, not run by an independent reviewer.
- It estimates ordinary research output from case context rather than replaying a full archived ordinary Mira memo for each case.
- It still lacks a true post-memo follow-through refresh.
