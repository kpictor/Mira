## Trial Purpose

This case tests the workflow's product evidence ladder:

`concept -> demo -> pilot -> paid_deployment -> repeat_usage -> expansion -> retention -> pricing_power -> margin_conversion`

CRM is useful because Agentforce is not just a demo story. Salesforce discloses ARR, bookings growth, usage metrics and product architecture changes. The harder question is whether these product signals are large and durable enough to accelerate total company revenue and justify a long-term thesis.

## Current Verdict

The workflow shows that CRM's Agentforce evidence is stronger than narrative-only AI software claims, but still not yet enough to prove a durable long-term product-led acceleration.

CRM reaches at least `paid_deployment` and `repeat_usage` on the product evidence ladder because Salesforce discloses Agentforce ARR, AI/data ARR, Agentic Work Units, token processing, bookings growth and Data 360 usage. However, the company-level revenue guide still implies only about 11% FY27 revenue growth, including about 3 points from Informatica contribution.
